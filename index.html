Feni Central High School

Feni Central High School, established in 1919, is one of the oldest and most esteemed educational institutions in Feni Sadar, Feni District, Bangladesh. Originally founded as Feni Central School, it was established by Principal Md. Mostafa along with Mokhbul Ahmed, Liakat Ali Bhuiyan, Khair Ahmed, Kefayet Ullah Bhuiyan, and Hemayet heUddin.
